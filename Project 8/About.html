Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you practice?

Currently the team practices on Monday's from 3:30-5:30 pm. We also offer optional practices on Saturday for kids who want extra help.

How much does it cost to join Bowling Club?

Currently bowling cost 5$ a week for plus a 65$ sports fee for the season. The sports fee pays for entering the tournaments.

What if I can not afford to pay the sport fee?

If you let us know before the fee is due we will do our best to set up a payment plan and help you fundraise Below is a calculator to tell you how much you still owe based on the number of tickets you sold in our fundraiser

When is there practice?

Starting this year we will be providing a calendar where you can find all practice, coaches corner sessions, and Tournaments. If it is not on the calendar, it is not happening.

Where is Bowling Club?

We currently practice at AMF Firebird Lanes. The map below will help you find it.
